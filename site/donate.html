What “free” means here

CertConvert is free and open source under the MIT licence. You can download it, use it, read the source and build it yourself, for personal or commercial use, with no feature limits and no licence keys.

Why donate?

CertConvert is built and maintained by one person. Donations fund the time that goes into new features, fixes and keeping it current. They are entirely optional — the software is exactly the same whether you give or not.

Other ways to help

  • Tell other people who wrangle certificates about it
  • Send feedback and feature ideas through the contact form or the issue tracker
  • Report any bug you hit so it can be fixed
  • Star the project on GitHub
